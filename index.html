About Me

Hello! I am a game design enthusiast, currently learning the techniques and art in the field of game development.

I believe that games are an art form about experiences, and designing games is an adventure exploring the infinite possibilities of the human mind.

Light & Shadow Puzzle

Light & Shadow Puzzle is a casual and therapeutic visual puzzle game. Players complete the puzzle by moving and rotating to change the positional relationship of light and shadow. The game focuses on the player's visual perception and image memory skills. The graphics are simple and delicate, often using color to convey emotion and mood.

A Rabbit from the Moon

Talking rabbits, mysterious lost syringes, hidden tunnels and laboratories in the island forest... These elements seem absurd, yet they are filled with captivating imagination. Players will take on the role of a young boy who embarks on an adventure with an extraterrestrial visitor during the summer vacation to solve a mysterious case full of puzzles. In the moonlit mist, the plots of friendship, conspiracy, and disguise intertwine. When looking up to the sky, it seems that all answers point to the white jade plate in the night sky.

PALE BLUE DOT

Inspired by a photograph and the writings of scientist Carl Sagan, I plan to create a game that explores the relationship between humanity and the universe. To express the grand and abstract theme of the universe, I have adopted a presentational style similar to drama. The game consists of four acts, each offering different interactive experiences and modes of engagement.

Starry Sky Reversal Board

Starry Sky Reversal Board is a two-player casual confrontational game set in the slime planet hundreds of light-years away. In this game, the slime have built a space paradise in low orbit, providing an entertainment venue for them to play. Players will take on the roles of two small slime and experience the joy of Starry Sky Reversal Board in the low-orbit space amusement park.
